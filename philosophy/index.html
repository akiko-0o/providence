"PROVIDENCE"は主に広告物のグラフィックデザインを作る会社です。
私たちは人々の記憶に残る広告物を作る事を目指しています。
広告物のデザイン・ビジュアルが見る人の人生にインパクトを与え、
その人が行動を起こすきっかけに慣れたなら

「あの時、町に貼ってあったポスターを見て衝撃を受けたんだ。
でさ、この道に進もうと決めたんだよ…まるで何かに、導かれたみたいだ。」

多くの人たちがそういう経験をすると思います。
そして、そこに私たちの作ったものがきっ欠けとなるなら
これ以上の喜びはありません。
これはものを作るすべての人に共通した喜びです。

その「きっかけ」をただ”偶然”と捉えるか”運命”と考えるか。
私たちはそれを天佑と考え社名を”PROVIDENCE”としました。
そして名前に恥じない、愛のあるものを作っていきたいと思います。

PROVIDENCE is a corporate enterprise that produces graphic designs.
mainly for advertising.
We aspire to the creation of advertising that will abide in people's memories.
Advertising designs and visuals can have an impact on the lives of the people
who see them and prompt those people to take action.
They may think:
"I was really moved by a poster I happened to see on the street at the time.
And right then and there, I decided on the course I wanted to pursue.
It was almost as ifI had been led by something. "
I believe many people have had this sort of experience.
We would be overjoyed if it was one of our creations that occasioned this sort of change.
This kind of joy is shared by all people who create things.
Is this occasioning encounter merely a chance occurrence,
or is the hand of destiny at work in it?
We like to think of it as a product of divine assistance, and so named our company PROVIDENCE.
We are committed to the creation of designs imbued with love
that will be true to the spirit of this name.
